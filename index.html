ABOUT US

S.S.Tourist And Transport Contractors is a well established travel/transport company. we have been offering wide range of services in the Corporation, industries, Institutions etc from the past 40+years. We are providing quality transport services to several industries and Institutions. We have vehicles with different models and seating capacity where we are able to provide transport facility according to the client's requirements. Presently we provide transport services to the following companies.

  • TDPS ( T D Power systems Pvt Ltd. Dobbaspet ) since 2010.
  • Triveni Aeronautics Pvt Ltd, Tumkur.
  • Karmobiles , Hirehalli.
  • Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumkur.
  • Incometax Department, Tumkur.
